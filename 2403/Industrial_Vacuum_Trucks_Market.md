These trucks are widely used in industries such as construction, manufacturing, oil & gas, and environmental management to ensure efficient waste handling and compliance with environmental regulations. The primary function of industrial vacuum trucks is to maintain site cleanliness, enhance operational efficiency, and minimize hazardous material exposure.</p>
