<h1>Armoured Bank Vehicle Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2023-2029</h1><br/><p>Armoured bank vehicle is an armored vehicle or truck used to transport large amounts of money, especially for banks. An armored vehicle is generally a multi-purpose vehicle designed to protect and ensure the health of the items being transported and the guards. They are usually custom built on a basic van or truck chassis with bulletproof glass, armor panels and a reinforced shell and cab. Armored vehicles are designed to resist robbery and hijacking attempts, able to withstand bullets from most pistols and rifles, as well as extreme levels of heat, explosions and collisions.</p>
