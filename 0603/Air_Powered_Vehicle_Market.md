 <p>Air powered vehicle, also known as air compressed vehicle, is a green vehicle that uses a compressed air engine, an alternative of the internal combustion (IC) engine, and works on the principle of compressed air technology (CAT). An air powered vehicle utilizes compressed air, a non-polluting fuel, as a power source in order to run the vehicle. Rise in cost of fossil fuel, owing to its limited stock and availability, and surge in demand for emission-free vehicles have led to the development of the air powered vehicle. The air powered vehicle offers long life of the vehicle, high energy density of compressed air, and low toxicity as compared to Others alternatives of the IC engine vehicle.</p>
