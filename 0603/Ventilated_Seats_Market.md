<h1>Ventilated Seats Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2023-2030</h1><br/><p>Ventilated Seats or seat ventilation is a feature that is built into the seats and it directs air through the seat unto the occupant for increased comfort especially on long journeys. Auto Ventilated seats function via several small fans in the seat cushion and backrest. The fans draw air from inside the cabin and into the seat. The air is then going through plastic ducts, an air permeable fabric and distributes it evenly throughout the seat via perforations in the leather.</p>
