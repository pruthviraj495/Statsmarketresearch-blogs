Coil springs were called a carriage spring, and sometimes it was referred to as a helical spring. A coil spring is that device which is used for storing the mechanical energy. Coil springs absorbs shocks and release after some time. Coil spring maintains the force between two contacting surfaces and make sufficient gap between them. Coil springs can be used as torsion springs and they are formed by winding a wire on the periphery of cylinder.</p>
