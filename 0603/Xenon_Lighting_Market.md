 <p>Xenon light is produced by lights that have high-melting-point glass envelopes and contain the xenon gas. Xenon light is a specialized type of gas discharge lamp, an electric light that produces light by passing electricity through ionized xenon gas at high pressure. It produces a bright white light that closely mimics natural sunlight. Xenon lights are used in movie projectors in theaters, in searchlights, and for specialized uses in industry and research to simulate sunlight. Xenon light can also be used in automobiles, which the xenon arc is used during start-up.</p>
