 <p>Multi-Walled Carbon Nanotube (MWCNT) Powder consists of carbon nanotubes with multiple concentric layers of graphene. This form of CNTs offers unique properties, including high electrical conductivity, exceptional tensile strength, and significant thermal conductivity. MWCNT powder is used in a wide range of applications such as energy storage devices, composite materials, and thermal management systems. Its structure provides enhanced mechanical and electrical properties, making it ideal for improving the performance and durability of advanced materials and devices.</p>
