 <p>Museum management software is a type of software that helps museum professionals and staff to manage various aspects of museum operations, such as collection management, exhibition management, visitor management, ticketing, membership, donation, education, and marketing. Museum management software typically includes features such as cataloging, inventory, conservation, loan, accession, deaccession, digital asset management, web publishing, interactive kiosks, online booking, point of sale, CRM, and analytics.
