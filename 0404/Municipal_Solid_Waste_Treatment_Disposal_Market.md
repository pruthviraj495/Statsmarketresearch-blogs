 <p>Municipal Solid Waste Treatment Disposal is a term that refers to the process of collecting, transporting, treating, and disposing of municipal solid waste (MSW), which is the waste generated by households, commercial establishments, institutions, and public facilities . MSW treatment disposal aims to reduce the environmental and health impacts of waste, recover valuable resources from waste, and comply with the regulations and policies regarding waste management . MSW treatment disposal can involve various methods and technologies, such as source reduction, recycling, composting, incineration, landfilling, gasification, pyrolysis, anaerobic digestion, and mechanical biological treatment .</p>
