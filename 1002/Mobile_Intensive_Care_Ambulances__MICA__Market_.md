These ambulances are deployed in emergency situations where patients require more than the basic medical care provided by standard ambulances. The MICA vehicles are typically designed with specialized equipment, including advanced life support systems, medical monitoring tools, and equipment for intensive care procedures.</p>
 
 
 
 <p>? </p>
 
 
 
 <p>The primary function of MICA is to provide comprehensive medical care during transport, which is crucial in emergencies where time-sensitive intervention can significantly impact patient outcomes.
