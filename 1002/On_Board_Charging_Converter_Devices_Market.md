<h1>On-Board Charging Converter Devices Market Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><br/><p data-pm-slice="1 1 []">The <strong>On-Board Charging Converter Devices Market</strong> refers to the segment of the automotive industry that deals with AC/DC converters installed within electric vehicles (EVs) and hybrid electric vehicles (HEVs). These devices enable the conversion of AC power from external charging sources into DC power to charge vehicle batteries efficiently. On-board charging converters are crucial for ensuring safe and rapid energy transfer, facilitating improved vehicle range, and supporting the growing infrastructure of electric mobility. These devices are widely used in passenger cars, commercial vehicles, and construction machinery, driving innovation in power electronics and vehicle electrification.</p>
