 <p>A Boat Trim System is hull accessories that can improve boat trim while running. Boats achieve their best possible running attitude at or near full speed and lightly loaded. Under slower speeds or when more fully or unevenly loaded, boats will ?squat? in the water with the stern down and bow raise. This hull attitude causes the engine to labor, the boat to slow down, and the fuel economy to decrease.</p>
