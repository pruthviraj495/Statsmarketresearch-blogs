<h1>Fully Refrigerated Gas Carriers Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2023-2030</h1><br/><p>Fully Refrigerated Gas Carriers Are Very Large Gas Carriers Equipped with Prismatic or Membrane Tanks That Maximize the Cargo Carrying Capacity of the Vessel and Are Often Suitable for Transporting Large Volumes of Gas over Long Distances. These Vessels Typically Range from 15,000 Cubic Meters to 85,000 Cubic Meters and Are Available in Three Common Sizes - 30,000 Cubic Meters, 52,000 Cubic Meters and 80,000 Cubic Meters. the Cargo Tanks Used on These Vessels Are Typically Made of Low-temperature Carbon Manganese Steel and Insulated with Polyurethane or Polystyrene, with Some Older Fully Refrigerated Gas Carriers Using Perlite As Tank Insulation. the Tanks Can Typically Withstand a Maximum Operating Pressure of About 0.28 Kg/c? and a Minimum Operating Temperature of -50?c. Gases Such As Lpg, Ammonia, Butane Propane, Etc. Can Be Easily Transported in Bulk on These Vessels.</p>
