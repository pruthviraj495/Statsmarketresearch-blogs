<h1>Palm Kernel Fatty Acid Distillate (PKFAD) Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><br/><p>Report Overview</p>
 
 
 
 <p>Palm Kernel Fatty Acid Distillate (PKFAD) is a by-product from refining crude palm oil. At room temperature, PKFAD is a light brown and semi-solid-state product of this refining process. Upon heating it melts down to a brown liquid. Palm kernel fatty acid distillate comprise mainly of free fatty acids with palmitic acid and olefin acid being the major components. The uses of PKFADs: PKFADs are increasingly used in the soup industries; as raw materials for the oleochemicals industry such as the manufacture of candles, toiletries and assorted cosmetics. PKFADs are also used by the animal feed industry. PKFADs have many other broad applications as well. It is used widely as food emulsifiers, as additives in rubber processing, the flavours and fragrance industries and pharmaceutical industries also use PKFADs intensively for their assorted formulations.</p>
