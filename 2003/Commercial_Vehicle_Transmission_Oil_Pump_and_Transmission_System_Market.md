 <p>The transmission oil pump is one of the most vital parts of the vehicle as it controls the overall displacement process of the vehicle. Displacement is the volume of fluid moved by the pump during each cycle. The transmission oil pump forces automatic transmission fluid (ATF) to supply fluid or oil to the hydraulic actuator with the valve body of the vehicle.
