<h1>Ro-Ro Vehicle Shipping Market by Player, Region, Type, Application and Sales Channel 2024-2030</h1><br/><p>Ro-Ro (Roll-on/Roll-off) vehicle shipping is a specialized maritime transportation service designed for wheeled cargo such as passenger cars, commercial vehicles, motorcycles, heavy machinery, and industrial equipment. Unlike traditional cargo ships, Ro-Ro vessels allow vehicles to be driven directly on and off the ship, reducing handling costs and minimizing potential damage. This mode of transport is widely used for both domestic and international vehicle shipments, serving industries like automotive manufacturing, construction, and agriculture.</p>
