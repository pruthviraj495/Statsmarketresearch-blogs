<h1>Plastic Railroad Tie Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2023-2035</h1><br/><p>A railroad tie is rectangular block of support used in the railways for rail tracks. Railroad ties are beneficial in transferring the load to the rail track ballast while holding the tracks intact. Composite railroad tie is produced from recycled materials such as plastic and rubber, which makes it environment-friendly. Composite railroad tie has proven to outperform traditional tie materials due to its properties such as resistant to several weather conditions and anti-corrosive. A composite railroad tie does not crumble or decompose over time. Composite railroad tie is also moisture-resistant and immune towards infestation by insects.</p>
