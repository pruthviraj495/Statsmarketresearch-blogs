 <p>The airbag connector is the electrical connector used to connect the vehicle airbag system. The airbag system in the vehicle includes electronic control unit (ECU), airbag sensor and airbag itself. The airbag connector is used to connect these components and provide power and data communication between them. The airbag connector is usually a special connector designed to meet the unique requirements of the airbag system. It must be able to withstand high voltage and current, as well as high temperature and vibration levels. It must also be reliable and safe, because any failure in the airbag system may have serious consequences in the event of an accident.</p>
