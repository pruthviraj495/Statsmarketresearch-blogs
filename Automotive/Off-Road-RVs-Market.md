<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Off-Road RVs, also known as off-grid camping trailers or rugged RVs, are recreational vehicles specifically designed to access and navigate challenging terrains beyond conventional roads. These vehicles are equipped with robust features such as off-road suspension, all-terrain tires, and durable construction, allowing adventurers to explore remote destinations with ease and comfort.</p>
