<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Car Exhaust Mufflers are components of an exhaust system that reduce noise levels produced by the engine's exhaust gases. They play a crucial role in enhancing vehicle performance, reducing emissions, and ensuring a quieter driving experience.</p>
