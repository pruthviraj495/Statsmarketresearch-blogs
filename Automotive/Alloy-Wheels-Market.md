<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Alloy Wheels are wheels made from an alloy of aluminum and other metals to enhance performance and aesthetic appeal in vehicles. These wheels offer advantages such as lighter weight, improved heat dissipation, and enhanced durability. With a blend of metals, alloy wheels provide greater strength compared to traditional steel wheels.</p>
