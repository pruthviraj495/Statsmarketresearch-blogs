<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Mobile Intensive Care Ambulances (MICA) refer to advanced emergency vehicles equipped with specialized medical equipment and trained medical personnel to provide critical care services during transport to medical facilities. These units are essential for ensuring the stabilization and treatment of critically ill or injured patients during transit.</p>
