Definition:

Automotive Infotainment OS refers to the operating systems integrated into vehicles to provide entertainment, connectivity, and navigation services to passengers and enhance the overall user experience. These systems allow for seamless integration of various multimedia features and applications within the vehicle's dashboard interface.
