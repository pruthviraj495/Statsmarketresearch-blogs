<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Motorcycle Engine Management Systems refer to the electronic control units and sensors that manage and optimize the performance of a motorcycle's engine. These systems play a vital role in monitoring and regulating various engine parameters to enhance fuel efficiency, emissions control, and overall engine performance.</p>
